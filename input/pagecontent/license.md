## FHIR Resources

The following FHIR profiles and resources are utilized within DHIS2 MIS:

- **FHIR Patient Profile**: Used for capturing patient data/ Enrollment of patients.
- **FHIR Observation Profile**: For storing health-related observations.
- **FHIR Encounter Profile**: To represent encounters with healthcare providers.
